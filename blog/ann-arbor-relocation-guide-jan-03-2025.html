üéì School Enrollment

Ann Arbor Public Schools Enrollment:

  • Register at Ann Arbor Public Schools Welcome Center
  • Bring: Proof of residency, birth certificate, immunization records
  • School boundaries determine which schools your children attend
  • Research before buying - school assignments impact home values
