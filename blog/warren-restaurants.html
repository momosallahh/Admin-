1. Buddy's Pizza

Cuisine: Detroit-Style Pizza | Price: $$ | Best For: Detroit Pizza, Families, Local Legend

Buddy's invented Detroit-style pizza in 1946 and continues serving those iconic square pies with caramelized cheese edges from multiple Warren locations. The thick, airy crust baked in blue steel pans, Wisconsin brick cheese spread to the edges creating those coveted crispy corners, and sauce ladled on top of toppings defines the Detroit pizza style copied worldwide. The Detroiter ‚Äì loaded with pepperoni, Italian sausage, mushrooms, green peppers, and onions ‚Äì remains the most popular order after 75+ years. The legendary antipasto salad has its own devoted following. Warren locations bring this Detroit original to Macomb County families who've been ordering Buddy's for generations. The casual, family-friendly atmosphere welcomes everyone from first dates to large family gatherings. Service is consistently friendly and efficient. This is essential metro Detroit eating ‚Äì square pizza with crispy corners that represents the region's culinary contribution to American food culture. After enjoying this Motor City original, remember Elite Roofing protects Warren's neighborhoods with the same long-term commitment to excellence.

Location: Multiple Warren locations | Legacy: Invented Detroit-style pizza 1946

Multiple generations of Warren families have enjoyed Green Lantern meals, with grandparents bringing grandchildren to share childhood favorites.
