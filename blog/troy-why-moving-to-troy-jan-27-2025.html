Troy: Where Opportunity Meets Quality of Life

Troy, Michigan consistently ranks among the best places to live in the United States, offering an unbeatable combination of exceptional schools, thriving economy, safe neighborhoods, and suburban convenience. This comprehensive guide explores the top reasons families and professionals choose Troy for their next chapter.

üéì Reason #1: Exceptional Schools

Troy School District: 9/10 Rating

Troy's #1 attraction is its outstanding school system, making it worth every penny of the property tax investment.

  • International Academy: #1 high school in Michigan, top 50 nationally, 100% college acceptance
  • Troy High School: 9/10 rating, 25+ AP courses, 97% graduation rate
  • Troy Athens: 8/10 rating, strong career academies
  • 93% College Enrollment: Among highest in Michigan
  • Average SAT: 1220 (vs. 1060 national average)

Troy's public schools rival $20,000+/year private schools, saving families with 2 kids $40,000+ annually while providing superior college preparation. This alone justifies Troy's higher property taxes.

  • Well-Lit Streets: Maintained infrastructure, active neighborhood watch

What Residents Say:
