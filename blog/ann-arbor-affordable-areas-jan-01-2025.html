Affordable Living Near Ann Arbor

While Ann Arbor's median home price is $400K, budget-conscious buyers can find quality housing in the $200K-$350K range through strategic location choices and property types.

üèÜ Most Affordable Options

1. Ypsilanti / Ann Arbor Border

Median Price: $225,000-$295,000

  • Location: Eastern Ann Arbor / West Ypsilanti
  • Benefits: Ann Arbor address, lower prices than central AA
  • Schools: Ann Arbor Public Schools (check boundaries)
  • Commute: 10-15 min to U-M campus
  • Housing: Single-family homes, townhomes, condos

2. Condos & Townhomes (Ann Arbor)

Price Range: $180,000-$320,000

  • Lower maintenance costs (HOA handles exterior)
  • Good for first-time buyers
  • Seniors downsizing
  • Areas: Near Pittsfield, South Ann Arbor

3. Pittsfield Township

Median Price: $320,000-$380,000

  • Ann Arbor schools (some areas)
  • Lower property taxes than Ann Arbor city
  • Newer construction available
  • Growing area with good amenities

4. Superior Township

Median Price: $290,000-$350,000

  • North/East of Ann Arbor
  • More land for your money
  • Growing community
  • 15-20 min to downtown Ann Arbor

5. Ypsilanti (Full City)

Median Price: $180,000-$250,000

  • Separate city east of Ann Arbor
  • Eastern Michigan University town
  • Historic Depot Town district
  • Growing arts scene
  • 20 min to Ann Arbor, 30 min to Detroit

‚úì Border Areas

‚úì Condos Instead of Single-Family
