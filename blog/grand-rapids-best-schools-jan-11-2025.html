üí° School Selection Tips for Grand Rapids Families

1. Visit Schools in Person

Schedule tours during the school day to see students and teachers in action. Most Grand Rapids districts offer open houses in spring for prospective families.

2. Consider Total Cost

Factor in home prices AND property taxes. East GR has highest taxes but best schools. Calculate your total monthly housing cost including taxes.

3. Look Beyond Test Scores

Consider extracurriculars, arts, athletics, class sizes, and school culture. The "best" school is the best fit for YOUR child's needs.

4. Check School Boundaries

Verify exact school boundaries before buying. Even within good districts, boundary lines can affect which specific school your child attends.

5. Talk to Current Parents

Join neighborhood Facebook groups and ask current parents about their experiences. Local insights reveal things rankings can't capture.

6. Consider Private School Costs Long-Term

$10,000-$12,000/year tuition √ó multiple children √ó 13 years (K-12) = $150,000+ per child. Compare to buying in a public school district with higher home prices.
