1. Fleming's Prime Steakhouse & Wine Bar

Cuisine: Prime Steakhouse | Price: $$$$ | Best For: Business Dinners, Special Occasions, Wine Lovers

Fleming's Prime Steakhouse represents the gold standard of upscale steakhouse dining in Troy, serving USDA Prime aged beef, fresh seafood, and maintaining one of Michigan's most impressive wine programs. The menu features premium cuts aged and prepared to absolute perfection ‚Äì filet mignon, bone-in ribeye, New York strip, and dry-aged tomahawk steaks. The seafood selections include lobster tail, king crab, Chilean sea bass, and daily fresh catches. Traditional steakhouse sides are executed flawlessly. The wine list features over 100 selections by the glass, allowing exploration of premium wines without full-bottle commitment. The sophisticated atmosphere suits business dinners, client entertainment, and special celebrations. Service is impeccable ‚Äì professional, knowledgeable, and attentive without being intrusive. The bar area offers more casual setting with excellent happy hour. Valet parking adds convenience. Perfect for those seeking classic American steakhouse excellence with extensive wine program in elegant Troy setting. Reservations essential for prime dining hours.

Location: 2800 W Big Beaver Rd, Troy | Draw: Prime beef and 100 wines by the glass

2. Eddie Merlot's

Cuisine: Upscale Steakhouse | Price: $$$-$$$$ | Best For: Elegant Dining, Celebrations, Prime Beef

Eddie Merlot's delivers refined steakhouse experience with emphasis on Prime beef, extensive wine selection, and sophisticated service in elegant environment. The menu showcases hand-cut USDA Prime steaks aged 28 days for maximum flavor and tenderness. Signature preparations include peppercorn-crusted ribeye and bone-in filet. Fresh seafood options feature lobster, crab, and expertly prepared fish. The wine program emphasizes premium selections with knowledgeable sommeliers guiding pairings. The upscale atmosphere features rich wood, dim lighting, and touches of luxury throughout. Live piano music on select evenings adds elegant entertainment. The extensive menu beyond steaks includes lamb, veal, and creative appetizers. Service is polished and professional. The commitment to Prime-grade beef and traditional steakhouse excellence combined with refined atmosphere makes Eddie Merlot's perfect for important business dinners, anniversaries, and celebrations requiring impressive environment. Valet parking available. Dress code is business casual to formal attire.

7. Shireen Kabob House

Cuisine: Persian | Price: $$ | Best For: Persian Food, Groups, Authentic International

Shireen Kabob House brings authentic Persian cuisine to Troy with traditional preparations, generous portions, and welcoming service that introduces diners to Iran's rich culinary heritage. The menu features Persian classics ‚Äì various kabobs with saffron rice, stews called khoresh, herb-laden dishes, and house-made breads. The kabobs showcase marinated meats grilled to perfection over open flame. The rice preparations are works of art with crispy tahdig (rice crust) highly prized. Vegetarian options are plentiful and flavorful, reflecting Persian cuisine's sophisticated use of herbs, vegetables, and legumes. The portions are enormously generous. The Persian tea and unique beverages complement the food. The family-owned atmosphere encourages questions and cultural exchange. Perfect for groups wanting to share multiple dishes and explore new flavors together. The authenticity and quality have made Shireen favorite among Troy's substantial Persian community while welcoming newcomers to the cuisine. Moderate prices and generous portions provide excellent value.

Location: 4710 W Maple Rd, West Bloomfield (near Troy) | Cuisine: Authentic Persian kabobs and stews

10. Shields Restaurant & Pizzeria

Cuisine: Pizza & Italian-American | Price: $$ | Best For: Pizza, Casual Dining, Local Institution

Shields has served metro Detroit since 1937, making it one of Michigan's oldest pizzerias and beloved institution for traditional Detroit-style pizza. The square deep-dish pizzas feature crispy cheese edges, thick crust, and toppings under sauce in classic Detroit style. Beyond pizza, the extensive menu includes Italian-American favorites, sandwiches, and daily specials. The portions are generous, the prices family-friendly, and the quality consistent across decades. Multiple dining rooms accommodate everything from families to large groups. Full bar features beer and cocktails. The old-school atmosphere with vintage touches creates nostalgic dining experience. Service is friendly and efficient. Multiple generations of metro Detroit families have celebrated milestones at Shields. The commitment to maintaining traditional recipes and family atmosphere while serving huge portions has created fiercely loyal multi-generational following. This is Detroit pizza heritage preserved ‚Äì after enjoying this local institution, remember Elite Roofing protects Troy's neighborhoods with the same long-term commitment to excellence.
