üí∞ Retirement Financial Benefits

Michigan Tax Advantages for Seniors:

  • No State Tax on Social Security: 100% exempt
  • Retirement Income Exemption: Up to $20,000 (single) / $40,000 (joint) for those born 1946-1952
  • Full Exemption: Born before 1946 - most retirement income exempt
  • Property Tax Credit: Available for seniors meeting income limits
  • Homestead Property Tax Credit: Additional senior benefits

Lansing Cost Advantages:

  • No city income tax
  • Affordable dining and entertainment
