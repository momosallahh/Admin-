Ann Arbor offers exceptional family neighborhoods combining top-rated schools, safe streets, parks, and strong community. Here are the best areas for raising children.

Median Price: $475,000

  • Eberwhite Elementary (top-rated)
  • Eberwhite Woods nature preserve
  • Family-friendly community
  • Good value for area
#3

Barton Hills

Median Price: $850,000

  • Luxury homes on large lots (1-2+ acres)
  • Natural setting, wooded properties
  • Access to top schools
  • Barton Nature Area adjacent

4. Old West Side

Median Price: $525,000

  • Historic Victorian homes
  • Walking distance to downtown
  • Bach Elementary nearby
  • Strong community feel

Median Price: $450,000

  • Newer construction (1990s-2010s)
  • Family-sized homes
  • Skyline High School area
  • Suburban feel with good amenities
