Snow Removal Services for Troy Winters

With 40 inches of annual snowfall, reliable snow removal is essential.

Service Options:
  • Per-Visit Plowing: $35-$60 per visit (typical driveway)
  • Seasonal Contracts: $500-$1,200/winter (unlimited plowing)
  • Sidewalk Shoveling: $20-$40 add-on
  • Salt/Ice Melt Application: $15-$30 extra
Recommended Providers:
  • Landscape Artisan: Commercial and residential, 2-3 inch trigger
  • Down to Earth Landscaping: Seasonal contracts, reliable service
  • Local Independent Contractors: Often best value, check reviews
Tips:
  • Book snow removal in October/November (best contractors fill up fast)
  • Clarify trigger depth (2", 3", or 4")
  • Ensure liability insurance coverage
  • Have backup plan for extreme snow events
