Everything you need to know about relocating to Beer City USA

Welcome to Grand Rapids!

Grand Rapids is Michigan's second-largest city and a thriving hub of craft beer, furniture manufacturing, healthcare, and culture. With a population of 200,000+, affordable housing (median $250,000), excellent schools, and a booming economy, it's attracting newcomers from across the country.

üå®Ô∏è Preparing for Grand Rapids Winters

What Newcomers Need to Know:

  • 75" Annual Snowfall: Invest in quality snow blower and winter gear
  • Ice Dams Are Real: Ensure new home has proper attic insulation (R-49+)
  • Winter Driving: Snow tires or AWD highly recommended
  • Home Heating: Budget $180-$250/month for winter gas bills
  • Roof Inspection: Critical before first winter - schedule with Elite Roofing

üè† Home Buying Tips for Grand Rapids

Critical Inspection Items:

  1. Roof Condition: Due to heavy snow, roof age/condition is critical
  2. Attic Insulation: Check for R-49+ and proper ventilation (ice dam prevention)
  3. Heating System: High-efficiency furnace essential for winter costs
  4. Foundation: Look for cracks from freeze-thaw cycles
  5. Historic Home Challenges: Heritage Hill homes need specialized inspection
