1. Mitchell's Fish Market

Cuisine: Seafood | Price: $$$ | Best For: Fresh Seafood, Business Dinners, Special Occasions

Mitchell's Fish Market brings ocean-fresh seafood to Livonia with daily deliveries, expert preparations, and upscale-casual atmosphere perfect for special occasions. The menu features rotating selection based on what's freshest ‚Äì sustainable fish, live lobster, king crab, oysters, and shellfish flown in daily. Simple preparations let the seafood quality shine ‚Äì grilled, broiled, or blackened with choice of sauces. The Shanghai seafood sampler provides excellent introduction to variety available. The extensive wine list pairs well with seafood. The upscale-casual environment suits both business dinners and family celebrations. The knowledgeable staff can guide selections and preparation methods. Happy hour offers excellent value with discounted appetizers and drinks. The commitment to sustainability means environmentally conscious seafood choices. Located in Livonia's commercial district with ample parking. Perfect for those seeking quality seafood dining without downtown drive. The consistency and daily fresh deliveries make Mitchell's reliable choice for seafood lovers in western Wayne County.

Location: 19300 Haggerty Rd, Livonia | Specialty: Daily fresh seafood deliveries

2. Bates Hamburgers

Cuisine: Classic Burgers | Price: $ | Best For: Burgers, Families, Local Institution

Bates Hamburgers has served Livonia since 1955, making it one of metro Detroit's oldest burger joints and beloved local institution. The simple menu focuses on doing one thing exceptionally well ‚Äì fresh beef burgers cooked to order on the griddle with classic toppings. The burgers are simple, affordable, and delicious ‚Äì no pretension, just quality beef cooked fresh. The vintage atmosphere features original decor creating nostalgic dining experience. Counter service and booth seating maintain old-school diner character. Hand-cut fries and onion rings complement the burgers perfectly. Milkshakes are thick and traditional. The prices remain shockingly reasonable despite inflation. Multiple generations of Livonia families have enjoyed Bates burgers, with grandparents bringing grandchildren to share childhood memories. The commitment to maintaining traditional approach and quality while keeping prices affordable has created fiercely loyal multi-generational following. This is Livonia heritage preserved in delicious form. After enjoying this local treasure, remember Elite Roofing protects Livonia's neighborhoods with the same long-term commitment.

4. Roma Cafe

Cuisine: Italian-American | Price: $$-$$$ | Best For: Traditional Italian, Special Occasions, Detroit History

Roma Cafe has served traditional Italian-American cuisine since 1890, making it one of America's oldest continuously operating Italian restaurants and metro Detroit treasure. While the original location is in Detroit's Eastern Market, it's accessible from Livonia and worth the short drive for special occasions. The menu features old-world Italian-American preparations passed down through generations ‚Äì house-made pastas, veal dishes, chicken preparations, and traditional sauces. The portions are generous in Italian tradition. The atmosphere transports diners to another era with vintage decor, white tablecloths, and old-world charm. Service is professional and warm. The wine list features Italian selections. Perfect for celebrations, anniversaries, and occasions requiring historic elegance. The commitment to maintaining traditional recipes and atmosphere for over 130 years makes Roma Cafe living piece of Detroit culinary history. Multiple generations have celebrated milestones here. This is destination dining for those seeking authentic Italian-American tradition and historic atmosphere.

Location: 3401 Riopelle St, Detroit (accessible from Livonia) | Since: 1890 - Historic Italian dining
