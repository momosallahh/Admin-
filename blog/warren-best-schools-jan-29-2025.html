üéØ Warren Consolidated Schools Highlights

Warren's Primary School District

Warren Consolidated Schools serves most of the city with a strong reputation for:

  • Career-Tech Education: Extensive CTE programs in healthcare, engineering, skilled trades
  • Dual Enrollment: Partnership with Macomb Community College for college credits
  • IB Program: International Baccalaureate at Mott High School
  • Advanced Placement: 20+ AP courses across high schools
  • Athletics: Competitive sports programs, state championships
  • Arts Programs: Award-winning bands, choirs, theater

üåü Special Programs & Opportunities

Career-Technical Education

Warren schools excel in preparing students for careers through hands-on programs:

  • Healthcare Sciences (nursing, medical assistant)
  • Engineering & Robotics
  • Automotive Technology
  • Skilled Trades (welding, construction, HVAC)
  • Information Technology
  • Culinary Arts

Partnership: Macomb Community College, local businesses for internships

Military Education Programs

Given Warren's proximity to US Army TACOM, several schools offer:

  • JROTC programs
  • Military career pathways
  • Leadership development
