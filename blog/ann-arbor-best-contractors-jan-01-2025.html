üí° How to Choose the Right Contractor

‚úì Verify Credentials

  • Michigan contractor license (check at michigan.gov/lara)
  • Liability insurance ($1M minimum) and workers compensation
  • BBB rating and complaint history
  • Manufacturer certifications for specialized work

‚úì Get Multiple Quotes

  • Obtain 3-5 written estimates for comparison
  • Ensure quotes are detailed and itemized
  • Be wary of significantly low bids (often red flags)
  • Compare warranties and materials quality, not just price

‚úì Check References

  • Request 3-5 recent Ann Arbor references
  • Visit completed projects if possible
  • Read online reviews on multiple platforms
  • Ask about challenges and how they were resolved

‚úì Review Contracts Carefully

  • Get everything in writing before work begins
  • Ensure scope of work is detailed and specific
  • Confirm payment schedule (never pay 100% upfront)
  • Verify warranty terms and duration
  • Include timeline with completion date

‚úì Ann Arbor-Specific Considerations

  • Historic District Commission (HDC) requirements for historic homes
  • City of Ann Arbor permit requirements and inspections
  • HOA approvals if applicable
  • Contractor familiarity with older home construction
  • Understanding of local building codes
