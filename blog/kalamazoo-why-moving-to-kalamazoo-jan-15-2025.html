üéì #1 Reason: The Kalamazoo Promise

America's Most Generous Scholarship Program

The Kalamazoo Promise, launched in 2005 by anonymous donors, provides up to 100% of tuition and mandatory fees at any Michigan public college or university for graduates of Kalamazoo Public Schools. This transforms families' financial futures.

Life-Changing Benefits:
  • 100% Tuition Coverage: Students continuously enrolled K-12 in KPS
  • No Income Limits: Every graduate qualifies regardless of family wealth
  • Any Michigan Public School: University of Michigan, MSU, WMU, community colleges
  • $700+ Million Awarded: Over 7,000 students helped since 2005
  • Family Savings: $60K-$120K per child in college costs
  • Property Value Impact: Homes increased 35% after Promise announced
Real Impact Example:

Family with 2 children: Buy $150K home in Kalamazoo, enroll kids in KPS from kindergarten. Both graduate high school, attend University of Michigan (4 years each). Promise covers: ~$240,000 in tuition. Family saves: Nearly a quarter million dollars while living in affordable home.

Why Families Move for the Promise:
  • Equivalent to $60K-$120K income boost per child
  • Removes massive financial burden of college
  • Children can attend top universities debt-free
  • Encourages academic achievement
  • Changes generational trajectories
