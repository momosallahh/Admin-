üí° Tips for Budget Home Buyers

Smart Strategies for Affordable Grand Rapids Homes:

  1. Consider Commute Trade-Off: Homes 15-20 minutes from downtown are significantly cheaper
  2. Inspect Thoroughly: Older, affordable homes may need roof/HVAC work
  3. Budget for Winters: Grand Rapids' 75" snow means higher heating costs
  4. First-Time Buyer Programs: Michigan offers down payment assistance
  5. Roof Condition Critical: Get professional inspection before buying
  6. Property Taxes Vary: Check tax rates by municipality
