Grand Rapids: A City of Exceptional Parks & Green Spaces

Grand Rapids offers some of Michigan's finest parks and outdoor recreation opportunities. From the world-renowned Frederik Meijer Gardens to the sprawling Millennium Park along the Grand River, West Michigan's largest city provides exceptional green spaces for families, fitness enthusiasts, and nature lovers. With over 75 parks covering more than 1,500 acres, Grand Rapids has earned recognition as one of America's top cities for outdoor recreation.

Whether you're seeking hiking trails, beach access, playgrounds for kids, or peaceful gardens, Grand Rapids delivers. This comprehensive guide covers the top parks and what makes each special.

  • Length: 15 miles
  • Surface: Paved
  • Route: Byron Center to Grand Rapids
  • Highlights: Connects to broader regional trail network
