Affordable Living in Kalamazoo

Kalamazoo offers exceptional affordability with cost of living approximately 12% below the national average. Combined with the Kalamazoo Promise (free college tuition worth $60K-$120K per child), families save dramatically compared to coastal cities while enjoying excellent quality of life, stable employment, and vibrant community.

üéØ Kalamazoo Cost of Living Overview

  • Overall: 12% below U.S. average (Cost of Living Index: 88/100)
  • Housing: 35% below national average (median home $160K vs. $420K national)
  • Utilities: Slightly above average (cold winters = higher heating)
  • Groceries: 5% below national average
  • Healthcare: Near national average (excellent facilities)
  • Transportation: 8% below national average
