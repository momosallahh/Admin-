How Much Does It Cost to Live in Warren?

Warren, Michigan's third-largest city and largest suburb, offers exceptional affordability for working families. With median home prices around $150,000 and a working-class culture, Warren provides quality of life at costs well below the national average. This complete guide helps you budget for life in Michigan's most populous suburb.

‚ö° Utility Costs

Average Monthly Utilities (1,500 sq ft home):

  • Electricity (DTE Energy): $100-$180 (higher in summer AC season)
  • Natural Gas (DTE Energy): $50-$200 (higher in winter heating season)
  • Water/Sewer: $60-$90
  • Trash Collection: $20-$30
  • Total Utilities: $250-$350/month average

  • Winter (Dec-Mar): Higher gas bills ($150-$200) for heating
