üí° Tips for Senior Home Buying in Warren

Buy for Aging in Place

Choose a home you can live in for 20+ years. Single-story, accessible features, and minimal maintenance will serve you well long-term.

Consider Condo Living

If lawn care and snow removal sound burdensome, condos offer maintenance-free living with community and security. Well worth the HOA fees.

Proximity to Healthcare

As you age, being within 10-15 minutes of hospital and medical offices becomes increasingly important. Prioritize healthcare access.

Get Thorough Inspections

On fixed income, unexpected major repairs are stressful. Get comprehensive home inspection + specialized roof inspection. Know what you're buying.

Right-Size Your Home

Don't buy more house than you need. Smaller home = lower taxes, utilities, maintenance. Comfortable 1,200-1,500 sq ft is plenty for most seniors.

Think About Community

Choose neighborhood with other seniors, active community, and social opportunities. Isolation is a real risk - pick area with built-in social network.
