‚ùÑÔ∏è Warren's Unique Roofing Challenges

Why Warren Needs Specialized Roofers:

Challenge #1: Heavy Snow Loads

Warren averages 38 inches of snow annually. Your roofer must understand:

  • Proper roof pitch for snow shedding
  • Ice and water shield installation (critical in Michigan)
  • Reinforcement for heavy snow loads
  • Attic ventilation to prevent ice dams
Challenge #2: Ice Dams

Warren's freeze-thaw cycles create dangerous ice dams. Prevention requires:

  • Proper attic insulation (R-49+ recommended)
  • Continuous soffit and ridge ventilation
  • Ice and water shield on eaves and valleys
  • Sealed attic penetrations
Challenge #3: Severe Storms

Summer thunderstorms bring high winds and hail. Quality installation includes:

  • Wind-rated shingles (110+ mph)
  • Proper nailing patterns (6 nails per shingle minimum)
  • Sealed flashing and penetrations
  • Impact-resistant materials in hail-prone areas
Challenge #4: Older Homes

Many Warren homes date from 1950s-1970s with:

  • Multiple existing roof layers (max 2 allowed)
  • Outdated ventilation systems
  • Aging decking that may need replacement
  • Asbestos shingles requiring special removal
