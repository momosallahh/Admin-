üí° Tips for Hiring Home Services

‚úì Vetting Service Providers:

  • Verify licensing and insurance (required in Michigan)
  • Check recent reviews on Google, Yelp, and Angie's List
  • Get 3 quotes for major projects (compare scope, not just price)
  • Ask about warranties on labor and materials
  • Confirm availability and response times
  • Get written estimates before work begins

‚úì Money-Saving Tips:

  • Schedule HVAC tune-ups in off-season (save 10-20%)
  • Bundle services with same provider (gutter cleaning + roof inspection)
  • Ask about senior/military/first responder discounts
  • Seasonal contracts often cheaper than per-service (lawn care, snow removal)
  • Preventive maintenance saves money vs. emergency repairs

‚úì Lansing-Specific Considerations:

  • Winter accessibility - ensure providers can access your property in snow
  • State employee schedules - many providers offer evening/weekend appointments
  • Older home experience - choose providers familiar with 1950s-1970s homes
  • Local references - ask neighbors and neighborhood Facebook groups
