1. Ice Dams (Winter)

Risk Level: VERY HIGH

Ann Arbor's 36" average snowfall creates ideal ice dam conditions. Prevent with proper insulation (R-49), ventilation, and ice/water shield.

Prevention:

  • Adequate attic insulation and ventilation
  • Ice and water shield installation
  • Professional snow removal when needed

2. Heavy Snow Loads

Risk Level: HIGH

Ann Arbor's heavy, wet snow can stress roofs. Flat or low-slope roofs especially vulnerable.

3. Wind Storms

Risk Level: MODERATE-HIGH

Spring/summer storms can reach 50-60 mph. Use high-wind rated shingles and proper installation.

4. Hail (Summer)

Risk Level: MODERATE

Occasional severe thunderstorms bring hail. Consider impact-resistant Class 4 shingles.
