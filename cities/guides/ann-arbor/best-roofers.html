üèõÔ∏è Special Considerations for Ann Arbor Homes

Historic Home Roofing Requirements:

Ann Arbor has several historic districts (Old West Side, Old Fourth Ward, Northside) with specific roofing requirements. Choose a contractor experienced with:

  • Historic District Commission (HDC) Approvals: Some repairs require HDC review and approval
  • Slate Roofing: Many historic homes have slate roofs requiring specialized repair techniques
  • Copper Flashing & Details: Preserve original architectural elements
  • Matching Materials: Replacement shingles must match historic appearance
  • Preservation Standards: Follow Secretary of Interior's Standards for historic preservation
