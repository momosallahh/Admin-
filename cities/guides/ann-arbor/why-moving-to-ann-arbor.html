Ann Arbor: Michigan's Premier Place to Live

Ann Arbor consistently ranks among America's best places to live, combining small-town charm with big-city amenities. üìö #1: World-Class Education at Every Level

Ann Arbor Public Schools - Michigan's Best

Ann Arbor schools consistently rank among Michigan's highest-performing districts with exceptional test scores, innovative programs, and 93% graduation rate. All three comprehensive high schools (Pioneer, Skyline, Huron) offer International Baccalaureate programs and 30+ AP courses.

  • 97%+ college acceptance rates at all high schools
  • SAT scores average 150+ points above national average
  • Award-winning arts, music, and theater programs
  • Strong STEM programs with U-M partnerships
  • Spanish immersion, special education excellence
  • $18,500 per-student spending (above state average)

University of Michigan - A World-Class Resource

Living in Ann Arbor means access to one of America's top universities (#3 public university, US News). U-M enriches the entire community with:

  • Cultural Resources: Museums, concerts, lectures, sporting events
  • Research Opportunities: Access to cutting-edge facilities and programs
  • Healthcare Excellence: Michigan Medicine ranked #1 hospital in Michigan
  • Continuing Education: Adult learning, professional development
  • Employment: U-M employs 49,000+ (Michigan's largest employer)
  • Libraries: Residents can access university libraries
