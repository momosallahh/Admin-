Why Ann Arbor Schools Are Among Michigan's Best

Ann Arbor Public Schools consistently ranks among the top school districts in Michigan and the nation. With exceptional teachers, innovative programs, and the influence of the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor offers world-class education from preschool through high school. The district's proximity to U-M provides unique enrichment opportunities, from university partnerships to cultural resources that few communities can match.

üéØ What Makes Ann Arbor Schools Special

‚úì University of Michigan Connection

Unique partnerships with U-M provide students access to university resources, research opportunities, dual enrollment classes, and campus facilities. Many teachers and students have U-M affiliations.

‚úì International Baccalaureate Programs

All three comprehensive high schools offer IB programs, providing rigorous international curriculum and college-level coursework that's recognized worldwide.

‚úì Advanced Placement Excellence

Ann Arbor schools offer 30+ AP courses with high passing rates. Students regularly score well above national averages on AP exams.

‚úì Arts & Music Programs

Award-winning music, theater, and visual arts programs rival college-level offerings. Many students pursue arts careers or attend top conservatories.

‚úì STEM Excellence

Robotics teams, science olympiad, engineering programs, and partnerships with U-M Engineering prepare students for STEM careers.

‚úì Diversity & Inclusion

Ann Arbor schools serve diverse student populations with strong ESL programs, special education services, and cultural awareness initiatives.

üí° School Choice & Enrollment Tips

‚úì School of Choice

Ann Arbor Public Schools accepts Schools of Choice applications for non-resident students, though space is limited. Apply early in the enrollment window.

‚úì Boundary Considerations

Your home address determines which schools your children will attend. Research school boundaries when house hunting - see our Best Neighborhoods for Families guide.

‚úì Magnet & Alternative Programs

Community High School and specialized programs require separate applications. Research deadlines and requirements well in advance.

‚úì Visit Schools

Attend open houses, tour buildings, and meet principals before making housing decisions. Each school has unique culture and strengths.

‚úì Check Real-Time Ratings

Use GreatSchools.org, Niche.com, and Michigan School Report Cards for current ratings, test scores, and parent reviews.
