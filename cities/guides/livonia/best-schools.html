Livonia, Michigan: Family-Friendly & Affordable

Livonia is a thriving middle-class Detroit suburb of 94,000 residents, offering exceptional value with median home prices around $220,000 - significantly more affordable than nearby Troy ($300K) while still providing excellent schools, safe neighborhoods, and strong community. This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about this family-friendly city.
