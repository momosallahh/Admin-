üí° Detroit-Specific Contractor Tips

‚úì Historic Home Considerations

Many Detroit homes are 80-100+ years old. Choose contractors with historic renovation experience who understand plaster walls, old plumbing, knob-and-tube wiring, and period architectural details.

‚úì Permit Requirements

Detroit requires permits for most major work including electrical, plumbing, structural, and HVAC. Your contractor should handle all permits - if they suggest skipping them, find a different contractor. Unpermitted work can cause issues when selling.

‚úì Lead Paint & Asbestos

Homes built before 1978 likely have lead paint. Pre-1980s homes may contain asbestos in insulation, tiles, or siding. Contractors must follow EPA RRP rules for lead-safe work practices. Asbestos requires licensed abatement.

‚úì Weather Considerations

Plan exterior work for May-October. Winter construction is possible but slower and more expensive. Interior work can happen year-round but factor in heating during winter.

‚úì Financing Options

Many contractors offer financing through third parties. Also explore: home equity loans, HELOCs, Detroit 0% Home Repair Loan Program (income-qualified), and FHA 203(k) loans for purchase + renovation.

üìä Questions to Ask Every Contractor

Before Hiring:

  1. How long have you been in business in Detroit?
  2. Can I see your license and insurance certificates?
  3. Will you pull all necessary permits?
  4. Can you provide 3-5 recent local references?
  5. Do you have photos of similar projects?
  6. Who will be the on-site supervisor?
  7. What's the timeline from start to completion?
  8. How do you handle change orders?
  9. What's your payment schedule?
  10. What warranty do you provide on labor?
  11. How do you handle unexpected issues or problems?
  12. Will you provide a detailed written contract?

During the Project:

  • Request regular progress updates
  • Document everything with photos
  • Review all change orders before approving
  • Don't make final payment until 100% complete
  • Get copies of all permits and inspections
