Troy's Premier Parks & Recreation System

Troy, Michigan offers residents an exceptional parks and recreation system with over 600 acres of parkland, state-of-the-art facilities, and year-round programming for all ages. From the popular Boulan Park with its splash pad and nature trails to the comprehensive Troy Civic Center featuring aquatic facilities and ice arena, Troy's parks enhance quality of life and contribute to the city's appeal as one of Michigan's best places to live.

üéâ Park Rentals & Events

Pavilion Rentals:

Most major Troy parks offer pavilion rentals for birthday parties, family reunions, and community gatherings. Reservations required, affordable resident rates.

Popular Rental Locations:
  • Boulan Park: Large pavilion near splash pad (most popular)
  • Firefighters Park: Pavilion with nearby playground
  • Raintree Park: Pavilion adjacent to playgrounds and sports fields
What's Included:
  • Covered picnic tables
  • Nearby parking
  • Restroom access
  • Playground and park amenities
  • Typically 4-hour rental windows
Reservation Tips:
  • Book early for summer weekends (March-April for June-August dates)
  • Boulan Park pavilion books fastest due to splash pad popularity
  • Backup rain plan recommended (no refunds for weather)
  • Reservation office: (248) 524-3484
