üí° Tips for Troy School Families

International Academy Application

IA uses lottery system for admissions. Apply during 8th grade (January-February). Students commit to full 4-year IB program. Consider student's readiness for intense academic workload.

Buying for School Districts

Verify school assignments before purchasing - boundaries can change. Proximity to top schools like Barnard or Bemis Elementary increases property values 10-15%.

Parent Involvement

Join PTOs early - they drive fundraising, events, and school improvements. Troy parent communities are highly engaged and welcoming.

Gifted & Talented

Troy offers comprehensive gifted programs K-12. Students identified through testing and teacher recommendation receive enrichment opportunities.
