Storm Damage FAQs

Common questions about storm damage and insurance claims

How quickly do you respond to storm emergencies?

We maintain a 24/7 emergency hotline and typically respond within 2 hours for urgent situations. For severe storms affecting multiple properties, we prioritize based on severity but dispatch crews immediately to all affected areas.

Will insurance cover my storm damage?

Most homeowners insurance policies cover sudden storm damage from wind, hail, trees, and other weather events. We'll inspect your damage and let you know if it qualifies for coverage before you file a claim. We have extensive experience with all major insurance companies.

Do I have to pay anything upfront?

No! For insurance claims, you only pay your deductible. We work directly with your insurance company for payment. Emergency tarping and temporary repairs are often covered by insurance and require no upfront payment from you.

How long does the insurance claim process take?

Most claims are approved within 1-2 weeks. Simple repairs can often start immediately after approval. Full replacements typically take 2-4 weeks from claim filing to project completion. We expedite wherever possible and keep you informed throughout.

What if my claim is denied?

We'll help you understand why and explore your options. Many denials can be appealed with additional documentation. We've successfully overturned numerous denials by providing comprehensive evidence and expert analysis. We can also connect you with public adjusters if needed.

Should I get multiple estimates?

Insurance companies often recommend this, and it's perfectly fine. However, beware of contractors who promise to "cover your deductible" or give suspiciously low estimates - these are red flags. Our estimates are detailed, honest, and aligned with actual repair costs.
