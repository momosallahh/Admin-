Commercial Roofing FAQs

Common questions about commercial roofing projects

How long does commercial roof installation take?

Timeline depends on size and complexity. Small buildings (5,000-10,000 sq ft) typically take 3-7 days. Larger facilities (20,000+ sq ft) may take 2-4 weeks. We provide detailed schedules and can work in phases to minimize disruption. Weather can impact timelines for some systems.

What's the best commercial roofing system for Michigan?

TPO and EPDM are most popular for Michigan's climate. Both handle freeze-thaw cycles excellently and provide great value. TPO offers better energy efficiency (white reflective surface). EPDM is more affordable and extremely durable. We'll assess your specific needs, budget, and building use to recommend the best option.

Can you work around our business hours?

Absolutely! We regularly schedule work during nights, weekends, or off-hours for retail, restaurants, and businesses that can't close. Some projects are done in phases so portions of your facility remain operational. We coordinate closely with your team to minimize any disruption to your operations.

Do you offer maintenance programs?

Yes! Our preventive maintenance programs include bi-annual inspections, cleaning, minor repairs, and detailed reports. Regular maintenance extends roof life 30-50%, prevents expensive emergency repairs, and maintains warranty compliance. Programs are customized to your roof type and usage.

What about roof repairs vs replacement?

We provide honest assessments of whether repair or replacement makes financial sense. Repairs work for localized damage on roofs under 15 years old. If damage is widespread, the roof is over 20 years old, or you're experiencing repeated leaks, replacement is typically more cost-effective long-term.

Do you handle insurance claims for commercial properties?

Yes! We have extensive experience with commercial insurance claims. We document damage, provide detailed estimates, meet with adjusters, and handle all paperwork. Our expertise helps maximize your coverage and streamline the claims process so you can get back to business quickly.
