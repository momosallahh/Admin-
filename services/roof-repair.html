Roof Repair FAQs

Common questions about roof repairs

How quickly can you repair my leaking roof?

We offer same-day emergency service for urgent leaks. Call us and we'll dispatch a technician within hours. For non-emergency repairs, we typically schedule within 1-3 days and complete most repairs in a single visit.

How much do roof repairs typically cost?

Most repairs range from $300-$2,500 depending on the extent of damage and materials needed. Simple shingle replacement starts around $300, while more complex structural repairs can run $1,500-$2,500. We provide free estimates before any work begins.

Will repairs match my existing roof?

We stock materials from all major manufacturers and have access to discontinued products. Our goal is perfect color and style matching. While slight fading differences may occur on older roofs, we blend repairs to minimize visibility.

Do you warranty your repair work?

Yes! All repairs include a warranty on both labor and materials. Warranty length varies by repair type - typically 1-5 years on labor and manufacturer warranty on materials. We stand behind our work 100%.

Can I just patch the leak myself?

While temporary DIY patches might stop immediate leaking, they often don't address the root cause and can void warranties. Professional repairs ensure the problem is fully resolved, prevent future damage, and maintain your roof's warranty coverage.

Will insurance cover my roof repair?

Insurance typically covers storm damage repairs (wind, hail, fallen trees) but not general wear and tear or aging. We'll help document damage, provide detailed estimates, and work directly with your adjuster to ensure fair treatment.
