Signs You Need a Roof Replacement

Don't wait until it's too late - watch for these warning signs

‚ö†Ô∏è

Age Over 20 Years

Most asphalt shingles last 20-25 years. If your roof is approaching or exceeding this age, replacement is likely needed

‚ö†Ô∏è

Widespread Shingle Damage

Curling, cracking, missing shingles, or granule loss across large areas indicates the end of your roof's lifespan

‚ö†Ô∏è

Multiple Leaks

If you're experiencing leaks in multiple areas, it's often more cost-effective to replace than repeatedly repair

‚ö†Ô∏è

Sagging Roof Deck

Visible sagging indicates structural damage to the decking and requires immediate attention and replacement

‚ö†Ô∏è

Daylight Through Roof

If you can see daylight through your roof boards from the attic, your roof has serious deterioration

‚ö†Ô∏è

Granules in Gutters

Excessive granule loss (looks like coarse sand) means your shingles are wearing out and losing protection

Roof Replacement FAQs

Common questions about roof replacement

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements take 1-3 days depending on size and complexity. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily routine while maintaining the highest quality standards.

What's included in your roof replacement?

Complete tear-off, decking inspection and repair, ice & water shield, premium underlayment, shingles, ridge vent, new flashing, cleanup, disposal, and lifetime warranty registration. All permits and inspections are handled by us.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes! We partner with leading financing companies to offer flexible payment plans with competitive rates. Options include 0% interest for qualified buyers. Ask us about current promotions.

What warranty do you provide?

All our replacements include manufacturer lifetime material warranties PLUS our exclusive lifetime workmanship warranty. We're GAF Master Elite certified, giving access to the industry's best warranty packages.

Can I stay home during the replacement?

Absolutely. Most homeowners stay home during the replacement. We take precautions to protect your property and work safely. You may want to relocate pets and cover attic items during the tear-off phase.

Do you handle insurance claims?

Yes! We have extensive experience working with insurance companies. We'll help document damage, meet with adjusters, and ensure you receive fair compensation for storm damage replacement.
