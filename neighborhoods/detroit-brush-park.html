Restoring Brush Park's Victorian Masterpieces

Brush Park is experiencing a renaissance. Once known as "Little Paris," this historic neighborhood features stunning Victorian, Queen Anne, and Italianate homes from the 1870s-1900s. These architectural treasures feature steep roofs, turrets, dormers, and decorative elements requiring specialized expertise.

We're passionate about preserving Brush Park's architectural heritage. Our team combines traditional craftsmanship with modern materials to restore Victorian roofs while ensuring superior protection. We handle everything from historic restorations to new construction.
